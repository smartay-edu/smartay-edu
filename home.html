About Smartay
+ 查看更多
Smartay Education was founded with the aim to improve education via technology, teacher training and study exchange.

Having been involved in various Department for Education Steering Committees, the founders have decades of experience in educational technology and service, working with national governments and school associations.
 
Our services have been used in schools and universities across the UK and abroad. We were recognised by the Mayor of London and were shortlisted
for the “Fasting Growing Business in the UK education Industry” award.

Today, we are actively looking to transform education by providing schools with great methodology and technology.
